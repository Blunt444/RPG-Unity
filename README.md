This Game is about a Knith who kills goblibs and retrieve it from there hands and give it to the kingdom.
The gold mine is where the goblins will be and it is where you have to capture it from them.
When gold mine is under goblin control it will act as a goblin spawner spawing goblins.
Apart from these random enemies will be spawned across the map(a part of map where you will free the mine from them).
After successful capturing the mine will be destroyed you have to rebuild the mine and by doing so you will get a cut of 20% from the mine production. With this gold you can buy weapons and upgrade/buy skills.
After capturing all the mines and upgrading skills along the path. The game overs. You can still roam but there will be nothing to do.
